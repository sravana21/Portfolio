About Me

Where Biology
Meets Data.

My name is Sravana Nambiar and I am currently pursuing an M.Sc in Data Science and BioAI at Digital University Kerala. My academic journey began with a B.Sc in Biotechnology from Chinmaya Arts and Science College, Kannur — a foundation that gave me deep roots in life sciences and research.

I am passionate about combining biological knowledge with data science to solve real-world problems. Whether it's understanding disease mechanisms through computational tools or applying machine learning to biological datasets, I thrive at the intersection of disciplines.

I enjoy learning new technologies, analyzing data, and improving my skills every day. I am especially curious about how artificial intelligence can be used in healthcare, agriculture, and biological research to create a better world.

My goal is to become a skilled data scientist who contributes meaningful, ethical, and impactful solutions to society — rooted in both scientific rigor and creative thinking.
